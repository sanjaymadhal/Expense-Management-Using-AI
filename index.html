Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Expense Management App?

An Expense Management App is a tool designed to help you track, manage, and analyze your personal or business expenses. It provides features like categorization of expenses, budgeting, reporting, and helping you maintain financial discipline

How do I add an expense?

To add an expense: Open the app and navigate to the "Add Expense" section. Select the category for your expense (e.g., Food, Transport, Entertainment, etc.).

Can I track my income as well?

Yes, most expense management apps allow you to track both income and expenses. You can record your income (e.g., salary, freelance work, etc.) and analyze your cash flow. This gives you a full picture of your financial health.

How does the app categorize my expenses?

The app automatically categorizes your expenses based on predefined categories (e.g., food, rent, transport, etc.). You can also create custom categories for a more personalized experience. Many apps use machine learning to suggest categories based on the merchant or description of the transaction.

How can I set a budget?

To set a budget: Go to the "Budget" section of the app. Choose a category (e.g., groceries, entertainment, etc.). Set your spending limit for that category for a specific period (weekly, monthly, etc.).

Can I set financial goals in the app?

Yes, you can set specific financial goals such as saving for a vacation, an emergency fund, or a big purchase. The app will track your progress and provide suggestions to help you reach your financial targets.
